Michael Stresing

Hey! I’m Michael Stresing, thanks for visiting.

I was born and raised in Vancouver, BC and moved to London to study at UCL. I spent 3 great years studying law (and 1 year studying it in Sydney) and graduated from UCL in 2018. My academic interests include Artist Rights, Privacy, Tech Ethics and Governance, and Employment law.

Since then, I’ve been working in the Legal Tech space, first by providing legal support in the early stage startup investment eco-system, and now as a developer - helping build the tools to make law accessible.

I’d love to hear from you (whoever you are) feel free to reach out on Twitter or Email :)
